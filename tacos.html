Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the late 19th or early 20th century. Mexican immigrants brought the taco to the U.S. as part of their culinary traditions. One of the earliest mentions of tacos in the U.S. dates back to the 1904 St. Louis World's Fair, where tacos were sold at a Mexican food booth. (source: smithsonianmag)
